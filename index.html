Visualizing 2014 ACS Risk Factors

Interactive Scatterplot

American Community Survey Behavioral Risk Measures

The American Community Survey (ACS) is one of the principal means by which the States and the Federal Government gain insight into the lived experience of Americans. Behavioral Risk Measures are a part of these longitudal studies.

Using a dataset from the 2014 ACS, I present an interactive scatterplot where one can choose the measures one wants to plot against each other. The chart will update and rescale its axes based on user input.
